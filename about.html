Our Journey

Founded in 2010 by passionate cyclists, BikeWorld began in a small garage with a simple dream: to make cycling accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for everyone. What started as a local bike shop has grown into a nationwide cycling community.

Our founders, Sarah Pedersen and Mike Rodriguez, shared a vision of creating more than just a bike store. They wanted to build a platform that celebrates the joy of cycling, supports sustainable transportation, and connects riders of all levels.
